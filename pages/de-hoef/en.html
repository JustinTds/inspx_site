Playgrounds need inspection too

Meet Elly, a fulltime playgrounds keeper at one of the largest playgrounds in Utrecht, The Netherlands. Dutch Regulation states that Elly needs to inspect the 28+ playsets on a weekly and monthly basis. Quite a chore when you have dozens of kids running around and the whole inspections being analog and therefore taking up quite some valuable time!

INSPX please!

Instead of running around with 28+ different paper forms, a pen and a photocamera, Elly started using INSPX on her favourite tablet and laptop. By walking around, taking pictures and speaking her remarks into her tablet, she’s able to breeze through her inspection chore. After ending her round, she finishes her report in the office and simply clicks ‘FINISH REPORT’. End of inspection!

Audit trail of inspections

In case anything happens or when a surprise visit of the Government Inspection Agency unfolds, Elly can simply open her mailbox and produce an impressive audit trail of reports, complete with pictures, dates and remarks and action taken.
