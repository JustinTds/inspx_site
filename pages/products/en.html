Inspect anything, anywhere

INSPX enables you to do standalone inspections or pre-filled inspections complete with customer relevant inspection data and information. Simply choose your inspection type and start right away. Additional information can also be sent from backoffice applications to INSPX prior to inspection. INSPX also allows for creating intelligent workflows and smart forms. INSPX synchronizes immediately over-the-air. No connection? No problem! INSPX can work offline as well and synchronises automatically or whenever you want it to.

Editing tools

Feel free to edit your full inspection on any device afterwards. Take pictures first and add remarks later when back in the office, working on a bigger screen. Fill out and add more relevant data to the inspection or report before finalising. Draw relevant circles or arrows, remark or annotate on pictures, drawings in the inspection. During inspection or afterwards. Redo parts, recalculate or edit parts or the whole inspection. Wherever, whenever.

Advanced reports

INSPX can generate fully customisable reports. Add tables, calculations, drawings, pictures or any other kind of static data to your report. Be able to add to or edit the report in real time or afterwards. Send parts or the full report to customers and/or back to the office via mail, XML or JSON.

Intelligent forms

Create or conduct simple or complex intelligent forms and workflows. INSPX is also able to handle complex calculations based on collected data during inspection. Pictures and drawings can be added and edited in real time or later on anywhere in the forms.
