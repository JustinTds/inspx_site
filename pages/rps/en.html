RPS Made by TDS

"One app, one device please"

RPS Group, a global engineering and consultancy firm had a challenge. They needed six outdated applications and three devices to generate one inspection. NEN 2990 regulations require the inspector to visually inspect and clear a sample for control of an asbestos demolition work. A signed hard copy along with pictures, drawings, calculations and remarks needs to be produced on site.

INSPX: problem solver

With INSPX they could feed two birds with one scone. It allowed RPS to limit the amount of applications and devices while speeding up the time frame for concluding an inspection.

INSPX met requirements and exceeded them:

  • – import of prefilled inspections from SIEMENS Unilab to INSPX
  • – build and conduct various types of inspections and workflows
  • – work offline while inspecting off the grid sites and locations
  • – be able to do in app Excel-like calculations
  • – integrated drawing tools
  • – edit and remark drawings/photographs
  • – advanced reporting tool
  • – integrated barcode scanner
  • – export of all types of inspection in several formats
  • – and many more

Gamechanger: advanced offline reports

With the ability to customize the design of their reports RPS is able to deliver many different types of reports depending on the type of inspections and wishes for their customers. INSPX provides custom report-design including tables, calculations and free styling. Pictures can be added to the report at will by way of insert or attachment. Drawings made during or after inspection can be added in the report by way of insert or attachment.

Happy

By using INSPX RPS Netherlands is now able to conduct thousands of different inspections per month. Asbestos, Chrome-6, Legionella to name but a few are some of the inspection types and reports that are generated in the field and reported to their customers.
