Who is behind INSPX?

Hi! Nice to meet you! We are TDS, the company behind INSPX. TDS stands for Traiectum Digital Solutions, a software production company hailing from Utrecht, The Netherlands. TDS and INSPX were founded in 2017 by Artem Maiorov, Justin Argante and Yoginder Rambocus. Together with a great team of engineers we started working on this ever growing project called INSPX. Our mission: to be able to let everybody inspect anything, anywhere.
